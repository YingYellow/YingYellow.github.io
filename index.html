About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Arlington, advised by Assistant Prof. Miao Yin in Noodle Lab. In my first year of PhD journey, I delved into parameter-efficient finetuning for large vision-language models. My current research interests lie in multimodal and GenAI, with a particular focus on efficient controllable and personalized generation.

Previously, I was a Software Development Engineer at Alibaba Group (Beijing), to build data platform services that enable efficient map data collection, processing, scheduling, and governance at scale. I received my M.Eng. in Computer Engineering from Hunan University, supervised by Prof. Xuan Liu.
