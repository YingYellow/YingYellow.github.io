I started this blog to record what I learn, how I think, and how I solve problems along the way.

In many cases, I find that writing helps me slow down and organize my thoughts more clearly in a way that speaking often does not. Instead of only keeping scattered notes, I want to turn them into structured posts that capture both technical details and my own understanding.

This blog will mainly include:

- technical notes from papers, and engineering practice
- explanations of ideas that I want to understand more deeply
- my thoughts on problem-solving and research, occasional reflections on learning

I do not expect every post to be perfect.
This is a place to think, refine ideas, and keep a record of growth.

Welcome to discuss!
